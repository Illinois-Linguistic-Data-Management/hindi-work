Vo laDkii has samay vo coat pehentii thii, isliye sab usko little red ridding hood bulaate the. Ek din little red ridding hood kii maa ne usse bolaa kii aapkii daadi kii tabiyat kharaab hai to aap ye khaane piine kaa samaan unke paas leke jaanaa. Lekin dhyaan se jaanaa kyunkii aapkaa raastaa jungle se hai to saDak pe rehenaa aur kisii se baat mat karnaa. To little red ridding hood chalii jungle se aur achaanak ek bheDiyaa ne usko dekhaa. Vo bheDiyaa red ridding hood ko khaanaa chaahtaa thaa lekin paas mai hii ek lakkaDhaaraa thaa jisne bheDiyaa ko dekh liyaa to bheDiyaa red ridding hood ko nahii khaa paayaa lekin aage jaake red ridding hood ne dekhe kii bahut ache phool the jungle mai to unhone bola ki mai daadi ke liye thoDe phool toD ke jaatii hu daadi ko acChaa lagegaa to jab red ridding hood phool toD rahii thii to bheDiyaa jaldii se unke daadi ke ghar pahunch gayaa. Daadi ke ghar pahunchke usne daadi ko pooraa khaa liyaa aur daadi ke kapaDe pehen ke unke bistar mai leT gayaa. Jab red ridding hood aayii usne bolaa “daadi aapke kaan itne baDe kyun hain” to bheDiyaa ne bolaa isliye taakii mai aapko achhi tarhaa se sun sakun. Phir red ridding hood ne bolaa daadi aapke aakhen itne kyun baDe hain to bheDiyaa ne bolaa taakii mai aapko achhii tarhaan se dekh sakun to phir red ridding hood ne bolaa aapke daant itne baDe kyun hain to phir bheDiyaa ne bolaa kii mai aapko acChii tarhaa se khaa sakun aur uske kehete hii vo bistar se niklaa aur red ridding hood ko khaa liyaa. lekin paas mai ek shikaar thaa jisne saarii aavaaz sunii to vo andar aayaa ghar ke aur usne dekhaa kii bheDiyaa bistar pe so rahaa hai aur aur uskaa peT bilkul bharaa huaa thaa to usne kainchii leke bheDiyaa kii peT kaaTii aur red ridding hood aur uskii daadi ko nikaal diyaa. Phir red ridding hood baahar se patthar le aayii aur bheDiye ke peT mai patthar bharke, uske daadi ne us bheDiye kaa peT sii diyaa.
